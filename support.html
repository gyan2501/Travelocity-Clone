Travel Alerts
>Change your Flight

Change of plans? No problem. We're here to help make things as easy as possible, you can either make changes yourself on Trips (recommended), or use our Virtual Agent by clicking the blue Chat now button at the top of this page.

Before you make a change

Be sure to review the Airline rules + restrictions in your itinerary to see what the airline will let you do and what fees they charge for flight changes.

Full-service airlines

If you booked:

1. In the last 24 hours: Some airlines let you cancel your flight for free. So you can book a new flight that works better for you and cancel your original – avoiding change fees while you're at it.

2. More than 24 hours ago: Go to Trips and log in to make changes yourself or use our Virtual Agent by clicking the blue Chat now button at the top of this pag

3. Basic Economy or Light fares: The airline won't let you make changes. Not sure if you booked one of these fares? Check your confirmation email or itinerary for details on your flight.

How to change your flight

Remember, some airlines charge a change fee per person – plus any difference in airfare – for a flight change.

Here’s how to get started:

1. Go to Trips

2. Select Change Flight.

3. Search for new flights and book the one that works best! You’ll also see an estimate of your flight change cost, if applicable.

Low-cost airlines

Low-cost airlines, like Frontier and Spirit, like to handle their own changes and cancellations. But we can help you get in touch. We've got lists of their websites and phone numbers right here.

Roundtrip flights with 2 one-way fares

1. Changing your flight is a different story when you have 2 one-way tickets instead of a single roundtrip one. For more on this, check out Roundtrip flights with 2 one-way fares.

2. If one of your flights is with a low-cost airline, like Frontier or Spirit, you'll need to contact the airline to change that flight. (They like to do that part on their end.) You can change your other flight with us.

Same-day flight changes

If you change your flight within 24 hours of when it's scheduled to leave, the airlines listed here might give you a lower change fee if you go through them.

You can check their websites, or give them a call, for info:

Alaska

American Airline

Delta

Jet Blue

Wondering why we can't offer you a lower change fee, too? Our hands are tied, unfortunately. The airlines require us to use the change fee as it's listed in the fine print on your ticket

Ticket transfers

We understand that plans change, and sometimes you just can't make the trip. Unfortunately, airlines don't allow you to transfer your ticket to someone else. There's nothing we can do about that.

Duplicate booking

If you have multiple itineraries for the same trip, you can usually cancel the extra itinerary online with no penalty fees. If it looks like you will be charged a cancellation fee, please contact us.

Name corrections

If you need to make a correction to your name, please contact the airline directly. Keep the following in mind:

1. The name on your flight booking must match the name on your government-issued photo ID.

2. Most airlines allow for minor corrections to misspellings, nicknames (e.g. William/Bill, Elizabeth/Liz), but do not allow name changes to another person.

3. Changing the name on your flight booking may incur airline change fees and require payment of any difference in fare.

>Flight booking mistakes and name corrections

If you spot something wrong with your booking, like a typo in your name, you have options for fixing it. Let’s look at the common issues that come up and what the next steps are.

Booking mistakes

If you find an issue with your booking or have multiple itineraries for the same trip, contact us for help. Most airfares can be changed or cancelled without penalty the same day they are booked.

Name corrections

If you need to make a correction to the name on your ticket, please fill out and submit our name correction form along with any relevant documentation as soon as possible.

Once we receive your information, we will work directly with our airline partners to try to find the best solution, and will get back to you within 48 hour

Low-cost airlines

To make changes to bookings on low-cost airlines, contact the airline directly.

Good to know

1. The name on your government-issued photo ID must match your airline ticket traveler name.

2. Most airlines allow for corrections to misspelling on tickets, and to nicknames (e.g. William / Bill, Lisabeth / Liz), but do not allow name changes to another person.

3.The airline may process a name change for the airline change fees and difference in fare.

>Book an accessible vacation package hotel

Many hotels have rooms for guests with specific needs, such as wheelchair access, braille, and TTY phones. Travelocity can help you find and book these hotels

To book an accessible hotel as part of your vacation package

Good to know

1. Sign in to your Travelocity account.

2. Select your vacation package and start the booking process.

3. On the Start by choosing a hotel page in the FILTER RESULTS sidebar, select your needs under Accessibility Options to filter your results.

4. Continue the booking process by selecting a flight and filling in passenger information.

5. In the Who's traveling? Section for the hotel, select the Special / Accessibility Requests (optional) link.

6. Select the Accessibility Options that fit your needs.
